Reblochon

Reblochon or Reblochon de Savoie is an appellation of origin for a French cheese produced mainly in Haute-Savoie and in some communes of Savoie. This appellation originates from the Bornes and Aravis massif, mainly the Thônes valley, and has spread to the Val d'Arly and the Bauges massif.

Munster
